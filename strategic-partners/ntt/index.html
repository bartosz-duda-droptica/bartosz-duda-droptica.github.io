Strategic Alliances

NTT and HERE team up to move the world forward with location services

Introduction

Our partnership

NTT (Nippon Telegraph and Telephone Corporation) and HERE have come together to combine ICT service development capabilities with industry-leading location technology.

Location technology has an immense potential to go beyond the automotive sector and empower the new generation of services and applications.

Together, HERE and NTT aim to improve the value chain across various industries and evolve in the area of location-based marketing.

Working together with NTT is also strategically important for HERE as it diversifies the company's mainly automotive stakeholder base and opens prospects in new verticals.

Both Mitsubishi Corporation and NTT recognize the enormous potential for location data and technology to fuel innovation, boost efficiency and sustainability, and ultimately make the world a better place.

Edzard Overbeek, CEO, HERE Technologies
