HERE & Microsoft Azure Strategic Partnership

Taking location intelligence to new levels with our Microsoft and Azure Marketplace partnership

Microsoft services and HERE provide a unique opportunity for innovation, growth and global footprint as well as security and trust.

Our combined industry leadership and technology will give you the confidence and reassurance that your enterprise solution will be able to use the latest cloud technology and security in your developer environment.

With the rich Microsoft ecosystem, you can easily employ a wide array of technologies (blockchain, AI/ML, Hyperscale cloud) directly from the Azure Marketplace.

Being a Silver Partner with Microsoft lets us uniquely serve customers with a better collaboration.

 

We look forward to seeing how our developer community leverages the power of location intelligence to enrich their enterprise and IoT apps with these robust HERE Location Service APIs.

Jeff Sandquist, General Manager, Cloud + AI Developer Relations at Microsoft

 

Our products

HERE Location Services (SaaS)

Access enterprise-grade location technology on Microsoft Azure, helping workloads run smoother for cloud developers, Independent Software Vendors and IT professionals.

With HERE Location Services (SaaS), you can actively develop within the Azure Cloud and in their developer environment to get a variety of product categories in the platform and marketplace.

HERE Location Services (Virtual Machine)

With Virtual Machine (VMs) enterprises, you can maintain your own dedicated server environments – without needing on-site infrastructure.

Our VMs have low latency, tight security and are optimized for blockchain, AI/ML and automation. Our highly flexible architecture addresses your security, compliance, privacy, and performance requirements.

HERE Tracking - Asset Tracking

Asset Tracking provides real-time visibility into your asset life cycle management and gives up-to-date information on the status and physical location of assets, both indoors and outdoors. Automate routine tasks and locate assets quickly.

HERE Tracking - Shipment Visibility

Shipment Visibility provides real-time visibility across the entire supply chain, both indoors and outdoors. Gain clear traceability that’s rarely lost, accurate ETAs and immediately remedied deviations.
