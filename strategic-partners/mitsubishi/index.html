Strategic Alliances

HERE allies with Mitsubishi Corporation to enable an autonomous world for everyone

Introduction

Our partnership

HERE and Mitsubishi Corporation (MC) aim to create new value through digital transformation.

As a strategic partner, HERE provides its platform portfolio to enhance digital transformation by promoting location services in logistics, retail, urban mobility and other sectors. But the collaboration doesn't end there.

While MC supports HERE's ambitions to accelerate growth in new industries and markets within the Asia Pacific region, the advanced location technology from HERE allows MC to work towards creating new solutions for middle and last-mile delivery.

Together with HERE, MC provides an industrial digital transformation platform that contributes to a better society by combining industrial knowledge and data from its 10 business groups* with a wide range of partners and advanced technologies.

*Natural Gas, Industrial Materials, Petroleum & Chemicals, Mineral Resources, Industrial Infrastructure, Automotive & Mobility, Food Industry, Consumer Industry, Power Solution, Urban Development

Both Mitsubishi Corporation and NTT recognize the enormous potential for location data and technology to fuel innovation, boost efficiency and sustainability, and ultimately make the world a better place.

Edzard Overbeek, CEO, HERE Technologies
