HERE & Esri Strategic Partnership

Improving business outcomes together with leading, global geospatial expertise

For over two decades, Esri and HERE have been a driving force in solving customer challenges using the untapped power of location data. 

With our combined geospatial expertise, ready-to-use solution tools and location intelligence capabilities, you can make faster business decisions and improve operational efficiencies while delivering on your customer and community needs.

Esri’s GIS software, powered by HERE’s fresh and robust location data, allows you to easily integrate with existing enterprise IT systems to deliver powerful, predictive location solutions and geospatial visualization capabilities.

 

Our strategic partnership brings together a combined 85 years of unparallel geospatial expertise, enabling our customers to unlock the full potential of location to solve geospatial queries and enhance situational awareness.

Robert Laudati | Director, Global Partners and Alliances, Esri

HERE & Esri Joint Solutions

ArcGIS Online

HERE supports the Esri cloud platform with street data sets to provide geocoding, routing, and high-quality cartographic display.

Learn more about Esri ArcGIS Online

World Geocoder for ArcGIS

HERE enables street-level geocoding for 149 countries as well as place-level coverage for other landmarks and cities.

Learn more about World Geocoder for ArcGIS

ArcGIS Business Analyst

HERE provides Streets, POIs and Admin Boundaries combined with Esri demographic, business, lifestyle, spending, and census data for map-based analytics.

Learn more about ArcGIS Business Analyst

StreetMap Premium for ArcGIS

Based on commercial street reference data from HERE, ArcGIS StreetMap Premium provides enriched street data for use in ArcGIS.

Learn more about StreetMap Premium

Data Appliance for ArcGIS

Preloaded with terabytes of global base maps and reference layers from HERE, Data Appliance for ArcGIS enables map services directly on your internal network.

Learn more about Data Appliance for ArcGIS
