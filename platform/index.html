A unique combination of capabilities in one platform

Mapping

Compile and merge your data with HERE data and create customizable interactive maps with fresh, accurate and global coverage for both indoors and outdoors.

Data enrichment

License from a huge library of datasets from HERE and industry leaders such as BMW, Accuweather and Ford via HERE Marketplace. Apply them to your data to discover richer insights that help make smarter business decisions.

Data analytics

Combine your own data and algorithms with large-scale stream data. Visualize your data and the results to explore patterns and trends for actionable insights.

Data visualization

Create interactive real-time maps easily without coding. View addresses as geocoordinates on a map and visualize your data to gain deeper insights.

Service and application creation

Build customizable and scalable location-based services with search, routing, geocoding, positioning and rendering functions quickly and cost-effectively.

Exchange and monetization

Exchange or monetize location-centric data across industries and geographies via the HERE Marketplace to generate new revenue streams. Anonymize your data and control what you share and with whom.

Data privacy

Prevent unauthorized access, usage, modification or loss of your data. Anonymize and aggregate shared data and let users control what they share with our simple and transparent data privacy tools.
