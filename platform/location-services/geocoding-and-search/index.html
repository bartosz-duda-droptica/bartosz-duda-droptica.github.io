HERE Geocoding & Search

Highly precise and relevant location context that saves time for your business

Get started for free Contact us

Streamline your search with rich location context

Inaccurate location information often results in delays, inefficiencies and lower customer satisfaction.

With precise and valuable insights on the location of addresses, places and points of interest (POIs), you'll be able and make better decisions for your business.

Minimize disruptions by translating addresses into geographical coordinates and vice versa to retrieve highly accurate location information. Search for places within our vast database of POIs and enjoy the flexibility of customizing the search infrastructure to meet your end users' diverse needs.

Benefit from one of the highest quality geocoding and search services available today.

Bring location intelligence to your application

Locate places accurately

Convert an address into latitude and longitude or vice-versa, by leveraging a carefully validated address and POI database that includes over 400 million point addresses in more than 102 countries and territories.

Get more value from your data

Use highly accurate and relevant search results such as administrative and postal code boundary shapes, high-precision postal codes, match quality levels and POI attributes to extract more location insights.

Customize your search

Add your third party datasets or logic to enrich or tailor your search experience to meet your needs. Integrate your own POI datasets and build your own ranking algorithms into the search infrastructure.
