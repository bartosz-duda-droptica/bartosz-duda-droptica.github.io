How HERE Parking works

Data collection

From HERE TRUE vehicles collecting data from on-street parking zones and off-street parking garages, to aggregating data from parking service providers and city.

Data analytics

Captured data is analyzed to remove duplicate POIs. We differentiate between garage entrances for cars and pedestrians. We use historical data from connected cars in order to create on-street-time-to-park estimates and parking availability index.

Real-time availability

In addition to relying on static and historical data, the HERE cloud captures information about free parking spots both on-street (using data captured by vehicle or road sensors) and in parking garages in real-time.

Under the hood

HERE Parking is made from our On-Street and Off-Street Parking RESTful APIs to give drivers a complete view of their parking options.

The HERE On-Street Parking API uses both historical and real-time data to predict where a driver is most likely to find a parking spot, how long theyâll be searching and what restrictions may apply.

The HERE Off-Street Parking API adds the availability of nearby parking facilities with pricing, restrictions and other useful information drivers need when choosing a spot.
